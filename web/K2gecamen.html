Usage

1. First buy a license and get a License Key.
2. After you bought the license, add my repo from here and install K2gecamen.
3. Open Tweak settings that can be found on setttings app.
4. When you use the tweak for the first time, please enter your license key and activate the tweak.
* You also need to reactivate the tweak every time you update the tweak (you don't need to enter the license key this time)
5. Add Control Center module of K2gecamen in Settings app.
6. Select the action to control capturing in tweak settings.
* If you have any question about the activation, feel free to dm me on Twitter (@m4fn3) or send e-mail to m4fn3q@gmail.com * One license can activate your 2 idevices. If you want to use it on more devices, please buy another license.

Features

Videos

You can capture videos in the background
It supports saving to both Documents folder and Camera Roll.
Also, it supports both front and back camera with 0.5x(Ultra Wide Camera) / 1x.
You can configure FPS / quality with your choice in tweak Settings
And you can select the action to start/stop capturing from LongPress or Double Press Volume Up or Down, Press Up+Down
You can always do so from CC by tapping on camera glyph too.

Audios

You can record audio in the background
It only supports saving to Docuents folder.
You can select the action to start/stop recording from LongPress or Double Press Volume Up or Down, Press Up+Down
You can always do so from CC by tapping on microphone glyph too.

Photos

You can take photos in the background
It supports saving to both Documents folder and Camera Roll.
Also, it supports both front and back camera with 0.5x(Ultra Wide Camera) / 1x.
You can configure quality with your choice in tweak Settings
And you can select the action to start/stop capturing from LongPress or Double Press Volume Up or Down, Press Up+Down

Hide the green/yellow circle in the status bar

K2gecamen has a feature to hide green/yellow circle during capturing video or recording audio.

More customize options..!

K2gecamen has a feature to select the location to save the files, and also to set the filename of the output files.
You can also disable the actions when the screen is locked.
And... you can have haptic feedbacks to make sure that you correctly triggered the actions if you want!
